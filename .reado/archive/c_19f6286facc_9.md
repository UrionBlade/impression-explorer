The comment attributes this index to "per hour of day", but a B-tree on ts does not back GROUP BY extract(hour FROM ts) - that is a function of ts, needing a full scan or a matching expression index. What idx_ts genuinely accelerates is the Black Friday date-range filter (WHERE ts >= X AND ts < Y). Also, hour-of-day depends on the still-open timezone decision (UTC vs a US local zone): if local, the expression is extract(hour FROM ts AT TIME ZONE ...), which a plain ts index also will not serve. Suggest re-labelling, and an expression index on the chosen hour expression if that query becomes hot.
