Boundary convention: the half-open even-odd test (strict > on y, strict < on x) counts a point exactly on a shared border as inside for only one side in the usual case, so adjacent states will not both claim it; where edge orientation makes both claim it, StateResolver.firstOrNull breaks the tie by insertion order. With float lng/lat, exact-border points are effectively nonexistent, so this documents the convention rather than flagging a fix.
